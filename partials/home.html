About Me

Though my background is mainly in print design, I take a strong personal interest in all things digital. I began teaching myself to create websites when I was twelve, and built my first fully functional site my freshman year of high school. I feel most at home creating UI designs and coding web pages, and aspire to be a front end developer.

Don't get me wrong though! I still have a strong affinity for print. I could probably spend hours mulling over typefaces and paper stock.

Outside of design, I enjoy reading all the latest tech news, listening to strange foreign music, singing car karaoke, and drawing/watching cartoons.

Fountain Street Fine Art

What started out as a senior year internship became a freelance position at the Fountain Street Fine Art Gallery in Framingham, Massachusetts. I worked closely with co-owners Cheryl Clinton and Marie Craig (along with dozens of local artists) to create a number of printed materials advertising upcoming gallery exhibits and other events.

Einstein's Dreams

A visualization of Alan Lightman's novella Einstein's Dreams. Digitally drawn and painted using Paint Tool Sai, with typfaces Benton Sans and Garamond. Winner of the Boston Book Builder's Award in 2013.

Massachusetts Early Intervention Consortium 2016

A collection of signage used at this years Massachusetts Early Intervention Consortium (MEIC) Conference. I worked closely with the Event Coordinator to develop a color coding system for the signs and corresponding rooms.

MetroWest Anti-Bullying Coalition

Multiple logos were submitted, but ultimately the Anti-Bullying Coalition chose to adopt mine as their organization's visual identity, used in their press kits and social media. The logo features a custom typeface.

Framingham Downtown Renaissance

Collateral for the Framingham Downtown Renaissance's Seasonal Events. This was a collaborative effort of a small group of senior Graphic Design students. Additional design work can be viewed in the Style Guide.
